How to Set up Lux AI

Setting up Lux AI is incredibly simple. Follow these three steps to bring intelligent conversations to your Discord server.

1

Invite the Bot

Click the "Invite to Discord" button and select your server. Grant the necessary permissions for optimal functionality.

2

Start Chatting

Use the !lux command followed by your question or prompt in any channel.

#general
!lux Hello there!
3

Enjoy AI Power

Lux AI responds instantly with intelligent, contextual answers powered by Google's advanced Gemini technology.
